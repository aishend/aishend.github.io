About

Who I am

Computer Engineering student. IT has always fascinated me — but so have other fields: logistics, aviation, the processes behind how things actually run.

Like everyone else, I'm in the middle of figuring out what AI means for all of this. Not ignoring it, not panicking about it — trying to find the best way to use it, understand where to invest my time, and make sure these questions don't become an excuse to stop doing things. I'm also paying attention to how it shapes the way we think, while making sure it doesn't think for me.

This is my digital notebook. A space to put whatever I feel like — projects, ideas, things I'm learning. Partly to have a record, partly to force myself to actually do stuff.
