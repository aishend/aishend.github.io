About

Who I am

I'm a Computer Engineering student. What keeps me going is not building things — it's understanding them. Operating systems, computer architecture, how software maps to hardware. The kind of knowledge that doesn't expire when a framework does.

I'm watching what AI is doing to this field and taking it seriously. My goal is not to resist it or defer to it — it's to understand its actual impact: what it changes, what it doesn't, and what it means to still be worth something as an engineer when the tools get this powerful. That requires going deeper, not shallower.

This site is my public notebook. Projects, progress, and the occasional thought worth writing down.
