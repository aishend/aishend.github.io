About

Who I am

Computer Engineering student. I care more about understanding systems than building things on top of them — operating systems, architecture, what actually happens when you run a program. Not because it's useful, though it is, but because it bothers me not to know.

AI is changing what this field is, fast. I'm not pretending otherwise. What I'm trying to figure out is what it means to still think like an engineer when the tools can write the code. My bet is: go deeper, not shallower.
